Short summary

Privacy First CleanLite is designed to be privacy-friendly. The app does not collect, transmit, or store personal user data on our servers. All cleaning and analysis is performed locally on the device.

What data the app uses

CleanLite operates only with files and resources on the device, and with limited system APIs needed to free storage and memory. We do not collect or transmit any personal information, identifiers, analytics, or usage telemetry.

  • Local file access: CleanLite scans device storage and app-cache folders to find cache/tmp/residual files. Deletion is performed only after the user confirms.
  • System stats: The app may read device and memory statistics (RAM usage, free storage) to present results in the UI. These values are read locally and not sent anywhere.
  • Permissions: The app will request only the minimum Android permissions required to access files or storage locations. Permissions are used purely for local operations.

Permissions we may request

Depending on your Android version and device, CleanLite may ask for:

  • Read/Write External Storage (or the Android scoped-storage equivalent) — to locate and delete junk files.
  • Run in background or show widget — only if you enable the Quick Clean widget (the widget triggers user-initiated cleaning).

You can always review and revoke permissions in your device settings. The app will handle permission denial gracefully and will not perform operations that require a permission you denied.

No tracking, no analytics

Privacy Focused CleanLite does not include analytics, advertising trackers, or any third-party telemetry in the initial release. We do not collect crash reports, usage metrics, or device identifiers.

Data retention

Because CleanLite does not collect or transmit data, there is nothing retained by us. Any temporary files created by the app for processing are removed immediately after the operation completes.

Third-party services

The initial version does not use external services, cloud APIs, or ad networks. If we add optional features (for example, cloud backup or advertising) in a future release, we will update this policy and include explicit consent flows inside the app.

Children

CleanLite is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If a parent believes their child has provided information to the app, contact us and we will remove it as appropriate.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the integrity of the app and any data stored locally on users' devices. Because CleanLite operates locally, the primary responsibility for protecting stored data lies with the user and the device's security settings (lock screen, device encryption, etc.).

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes to the app or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will post the new policy here and update the "Effective date" above.
